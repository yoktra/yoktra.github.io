Connected Car Solutions |
VT-100 for any model car

VT-100 is an advanced car tracking solution. Yoktra App on your mobile combines with VT-100 OBD (On-board diagnostics) GPS device in your car to make your car a smart car and you a smarter driver. Just plug VT-100 device to your car and make your car always connected to the cloud. Yoktra App on your mobile connects to your car's data in the cloud and brings you all the insights at your finger-tips.
